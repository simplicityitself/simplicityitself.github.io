{% include section-header.html class="alt" title="Microservices" %}

Microservices is an architecture style focused on responding to the most important property of modern software, the force of change! Because microservices actively design for change, engineering teams can react to business needs in a more confident manner, increasing comprehension and trust between all the stakeholders.

We can help you to build your systems, train you how to use Microservices, and provide oversight and board level advice to adopt the most change ready software architecture.

{% include section-header.html class="alt" title="Continuous Delivery" %}

Continuous Delivery and DevOps are tightly intertwined process and organisation changes that enable your software teams to deliver software faster, and with greater precision.

Our consultants and delivery teams can enable you to adopt these new processes by working with your board, delivery teams and other stakeholders.

{% include section-header.html class="alt" title="Event Architectures" %}

Your data is the lifeblood of your software. Software with no data has no value.

Event Architectures enable change and resilience at the level of data like no other. Whether design time, in production, long tail maintenace or at other points, data structured into event streams can be used and re-used with ease. We can guide you on how to adopt this new paradigm, show you the possibilities in data science and machine learning this enables.

{% include section-header.html class="alt" title="Cloud Native Infrastructure" %}

Cloud Native describes making your approach to software development work with cloud services in the most natural way possible. Covering containerisation, Docker, AWS Lambda, Mesos, Kubernetes, other orchestration platform.

Adopting a Cloud Native posture for your organisation will enable you to take full advantage of the modern coud, and enable you to deploy more quickly, with greater confidence, and create a more resilient environment to work within.
